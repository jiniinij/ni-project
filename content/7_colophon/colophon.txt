Title: Colophon

Description: <p>Launched in 2024, Project NI is a contemporary music collective exploring human 'Native Intelligence' in the age of Artificial Intelligence (AI). We directly sample extended techniques and improvisations, reinterpreting these sounds not as mere data, but as 'preserved acts of performance' and new creative materials. This experiment overcomes the physical constraints of the creative environment while shedding new light on the value of instrumental music and restoring its sonic presence in the digital age.<br><br>Copyright for all audio samples provided on this website is held by Project NI. Attribution (Mandatory) Any work or project utilizing these samples must include the source attribution as follows:'Source: Project NI' or 'Audio Sample Source: Project NI'<br><br>Here is the text with all double quotation marks converted to single quotation marks:<br>File names follow the structure: &lt;NI&gt;&lt;Instrument&gt;&lt;Technique&gt;<br>For sections other than the Instrument, abbreviations are indicated with a period (.).<br>File names do not contain spaces.<br>When combining two or more techniques, they are connected with a plus sign (+).<br>If a single sample contains multiple pitches, they are connected with a hyphen (-).<br>Samples with strong improvisational elements are marked as 'Impro.' in the &lt;Technique&gt; section.<br>Samples using similar techniques are distinguished by 'V' (Version) followed by a number (e.g., V1, V2, V3). Numbers are assigned arbitrarily, regardless of length or form.<br>Multi-microphone recordings of the same sound are distinguished by 'Mic' (Microphone) followed by a number (e.g., Mic1, Mic2, Mic3). Numbers are assigned arbitrarily, and for certain samples, the file durations may not perfectly match.</p><p>Design &amp; Code by So Jin Park</p><p>Copyright 2024. Project NI.
