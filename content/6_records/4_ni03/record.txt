Description: <p>The 1st Showcase: Exploring New Instrumental Music Project NI's first showcase was presented as a screening of works utilizing recorded instrument samples, accompanied by commentary from the composers. Five new works by Hyuk Son, Sumin An, Taehyun Ha, Sunghoon Bae, and Seokhee Lee were premiered, demonstrating a commitment to exploring new directions in instrumental music through sampling. Reflecting the diverse backgrounds and distinct styles of the composers, the performance featured a wide spectrum of sounds, ranging from game music to experimental contemporary pieces.</p>
