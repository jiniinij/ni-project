Description: <p>The 2nd Showcase: From Fixed Sounds to Improvisation The second showcase presented previously released works by Sumin An and Hyuk Son, alongside five new pieces by Seokhee Lee, Sunghoon Bae, Taehyun Ha, and Chaerin Park. While maintaining the format of composer commentary, this performance distinguished itself by focusing on 'improvisation.' Moving beyond fixed sounds, the works incorporated samples containing improvisational elements. Furthermore, the event expanded into live performance, highlighted by Taehyun Ha’s live oboe improvisation on stage.</p>
