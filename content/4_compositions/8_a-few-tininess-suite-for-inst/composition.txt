Description: <p>This project presents a suite composed from recorded sounds of various instrumental groups. Each movement is built around the timbres of specific instruments, focusing on shaping a distinct musical identity within a limited palette of sonic materials.</p><p>Movements I and II each employ a single instrumental group and a confined set of sources, heightening the focus on timbral depth and expressive nuance.</p><p>Movement III, by contrast, introduces a broader spectrum of instruments and sounds. This expansion is not merely additive, but rather a means of achieving contrast and equilibrium within the overall architecture through the interplay of opposing elements.</p>
