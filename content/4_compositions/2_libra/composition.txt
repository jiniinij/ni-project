Description: <p>Samples of contemporary instrumental techniques were edited and processed, then woven subtly yet distinctly into the game’s musical fabric. Some sounds were preserved in near-original form, with slight adjustments to space, tone, and blend to integrate smoothly with other instruments and textures. Others were treated more experimentally, applying production-based approaches to explore possibilities beyond conventional instrumental practice.</p><p>By combining both unaltered and heavily processed materials, the work highlights the potential of contemporary instrumental techniques—rarely heard in media music—within a new sonic context. It also reflects on creative strategies in an era defined by rapid technological advancement and the rise of artificial intelligence.</p>
