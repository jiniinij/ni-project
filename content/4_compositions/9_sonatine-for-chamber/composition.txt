Description: <p>This work is constructed by integrating contemporary sonic elements into the classical framework of a Sonatine. Throughout the compositional process, a fundamental question persistently arose: 'Has music truly evolved with the passage of time?' This inquiry naturally served as the genesis of the piece.</p><p>Furthermore, the work is permeated by questions regarding whether auditory experience can transcend the visual, and whether avant-garde music still retains valid significance today. In an era where technology and media have expanded, I sought to re-examine the sensory position that music occupies. I believe that music should not remain an isolated experiment but must ultimately move towards a direction where it can be shared and enjoyed by all.</p><p>Navigating the gaps between form and sound, past and present, this piece quietly poses a question to the listener: 'For whom does this music exist?'</p>
