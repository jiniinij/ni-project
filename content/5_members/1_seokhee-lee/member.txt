Introduction: <p>Seokhee Lee (hnei) is a composer and writer active in South Korea and Germany, whose work is based on contemporary music. He transcends the simple sensory perception of sound, arranging and combining sounds within various contexts and environments to reveal and expand their essence. In this process, he re-explores not only the physical phenomena of sound elements but also their cognitive and aesthetic dimensions, proposing a new direction for auditory art. In his music, individual sounds do not exist independently; they are reflected and refracted like waves, placed within a microscopically and macroscopically integrated network of relationships. Simultaneously, the sounds structured within a controlled arrangement pose fundamental questions to conventional musical language and methods of appreciation, enabling a more complex dimension of listening.</p>
