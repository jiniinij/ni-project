Introduction: <p>Green Hour is a musical collective dedicated to bringing vibrant green energy to the gray-toned, fast-paced lives of modern people. Led by flutist Hyewon Kim and guitarist Seoin Lee, Green Hour draws inspiration from nature and everyday elements, reinterpreting them through the lens of contemporary jazz. Their unique sound is defined by the fusion of the flute’s clear, rhythmic melodies and the smooth tones of jazz guitar, further enriched by a deep bass and the free-spirited rhythms of the drums. Green Hour’s music offers a moment of restful sanctuary—like a quiet stroll through a forest—infusing new vitality into the weary lives of contemporary listeners.</p>
